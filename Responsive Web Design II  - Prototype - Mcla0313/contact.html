As creatives here at CameraClass, we strive to show how we are just like all of you! We were a group of IMD student that had just graduated from Algonquin College and started this company with a long road ahead.

We started 6 years ago and the dream we had for this company was to create a hub where all can feel welcome weather they had questions about their equipment to editing questions we wanted to provide a space where all felt welcome!

We couldn’t have done it without your support. So when we come into work every day we put our 100% into our work to give all of you creatives a hub where we can come together as a community to say thank you for your continued support.
